About

Hi, my name is Neha. A few months ago, I was trying to find a last-minute outfit for my brother's wedding and failing miserably. Every website I came across either had dated styles or horror stories for reviews. After agonizing for a week, I finally bit the bullet and bought a lehenga, never to hear from the retailer again. That's when I came up with the idea for Laado: South Asian fashion you can trust.
