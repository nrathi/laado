About

Hi, my name is Neha. A few months ago, I was trying to find an outfit for a wedding and failing miserably. Every website I came across either had dated styles or horror stories for reviews. After agonizing for a week, I finally bit the bullet and bought a lehenga, never to hear from the retailer again. That's when I knew something had to change. Laado is the Hindi word for "pampered", and I hope to make you feel the same.
