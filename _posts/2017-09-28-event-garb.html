So you've just been invited to an Indian event... now what? I recently described all the traditional indian outfits in my Instagram, but let's recap. For ladies, they are:

SALWAR SUIT
ANARKALI
LEHENGA
SARI
  • Salwar suit – a staple consisting of a long embroidered tunic over pants.
  • Anarkali – similar to a suit, except the top has more of a gown flare.
  • Lehenga – an embellished crop top paired with a long, pleated skirt.
  • Sari – yet another staple, in which fabric is draped around the waist and over the shoulder of a blouse.

And for men:

KURTA
JODHPURI
NEHRU VEST
SHERWANI
  • Kurta – a long shirt accompanied by a choice of pants (pajamas, churidars, etc).
  • Jodhpuri – a western style suit with a coat and trouser.
  • Nehru vest – inspired by the Jodhpuri, a hip-length coat with a mandarin collar.
  • Sherwani – effectively a longer Nehru jacket; very similar to a British frock.
